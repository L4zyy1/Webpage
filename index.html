Temperature registering page

The climate of Finland displays features of both maritime and continental climates, depending on the direction of air flow.
Considering its northern location, the mean temperature in Finland is several degrees higher than in most other areas at these latitudes, e.g. Siberia and southern Greenland.

Temperatures can be measures in:

  • Celsius 0°C
  • Fahrenheit 32°F
  • Kelvin 273.15K
   F 1.8 * C = 32
